Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before we talk. Still unsure? Book a free call.

Do I need to be technical to work with you?

Not at all. I handle all the technical heavy lifting. You just need to know your business process and pain points — I'll translate that into working automations and explain everything in plain language.

Will this work with the tools I already use?

Almost certainly yes. n8n and Make connect to 500+ apps including Gmail, Slack, Notion, Airtable, Shopify, HubSpot, and virtually any tool with an API. We'll confirm compatibility in the discovery call.

How long does a typical project take?

A focused automation sprint takes about 1 week. A full AI agent or voice bot build runs 2–4 weeks. Enterprise AIOS deployments are scoped per project. You'll get a clear timeline before any work starts.

What happens if something breaks after launch?

Every project includes post-launch support — 7 days on Starter, 30 days on Full-Stack AI builds. I'm also available for retainer arrangements if you want ongoing monitoring and iterative improvements.

Is my data safe? Will it go to third-party AI services?

It depends on your requirements. For sensitive data, I specialise in local LLM stacks (Ollama + private models) where nothing leaves your servers. For other projects, we use enterprise-tier API services with strong data handling policies.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance or retainers?

Yes. Monthly retainers are available for clients who want regular updates, new workflow additions, model fine-tuning, or proactive monitoring. Rates start at $500/month. We can discuss this after your first project.
